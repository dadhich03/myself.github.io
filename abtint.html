:- Major Projects

Master Slave Robot

A master-slave robot system consists of a controlling unit (master) and one or more controlled units (slaves). The master unit transmits commands and controls the movements of the slave unit(s). This hierarchical setup enables precise control and coordination, making it useful in various applications, including surgery, manufacturing, and exploration.

Aerodynamics

Aerodynamics is the study of how gases interact with objects in motion, particularly air and its effects on vehicles, aircraft, and structures. It involves analyzing forces such as lift, drag, and thrust to optimize design and performance. Aerodynamics plays a crucial role in fields like aerospace engineering, automotive design, and sports equipment development.
